Insurance

Insurance Provider

Insurance Companies in India are recognized by the IRDA – Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India, the statutory body promoting and regulating various types of insurance companies in India.

The insurance industry comprises a total of 57 insurance companies in India. Life Insurance Corporation of India is the only public sector company among the life insurers. General Insurance Corporation of India is the only reinsurer in India recognized by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority.

We provide different types of Insurances like Health insurance, General Insurance, life insurance, Commercial Insurance, Term Insurance

Loan Provider

The term loan refers to a sort of credit,wherein a amount of money is lent to every other party in exchange for future repayment of the value or principal amount. In many cases, the lender also provides interest or finance charges to the principal value which the borrower need to pay off similarly to the principal balance.

Loans can be for a specific, one-time amount, or they will be available as an open-ended line of credit as much as a specified limit. Loans are available in many forms such as secured, unsecured, commercial, and personal loans.
