Personal loans are a form of unsecured loan popular to meet immediate needs. They are versatile in nature, so they can be used for various purposes such as weddings, home renovations, travel, and more. Also, there are no restrictions on the amount borrowed and they can be used for any purpose you want. In fact, a personal loan can help you build your credit faster than other forms of credit. This is because of the risk involved because it is unsecured.

No collateral required

One of the advantages of an online personal loan is that it is unsecured. This means that you do not have to deposit any collateral for your loan. Although this can be a reason for your interest rate, you can get a low-interest personal loan easily if you meet the eligibility requirements.

Minimal Documentation

In the age of digitization, the verification and documentation process has now been shortened. This is true for an instant personal loans as they pay off quickly and require few documents for approval.

Easy Approval

You can get personal loans that can be approved quickly and easily. That is why it is the best choice in times of financial distress. In addition, the process has been digitized; thereby reducing the time in the verification process.

Multipurpose in nature

A personal loan can be used for anything except investments and illegal activities. Aside from that, it is versatile in nature and can be used for anything. Other forms of credit, when taken out, serve a specific purpose and can only be used for the same purpose, for example, a mortgage loan is used to buy a house while an auto loan is used to buy a car. However, a personal loan can be taken out to offset immediate needs.

Personal loans are issued by most banks/NBFCs, but with different eligibility requirements. However, there are certain criteria that are common to all financial institutions. The eligibility criteria for obtaining an online personal loan approval for employees and self-employed are:

Salaried
  • Age: 18 years to 60 years
  • Credit score: 750 or above with a good credit history
  • Minimum Net Income (Monthly): ₹15,000 to ₹20,000
  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹50 lacs depending on the credit profile
  • Work Experience: Employed at current company for at least 6/12 months
Self-employed
  • Age: 21 years to 65 years
  • Credit score: 750 or above with a good credit history
  • Minimum Net Income (Monthly): ₹15,000 to ₹20,000
  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹50 lacs depending on the credit profile
  • Work Experience: Business tenure of at least 3 years (continuous)
