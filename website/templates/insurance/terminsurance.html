Term life insurance is the simplest and purest form of life insurance, offering financial protection to the policyholder for a fixed period of time in exchange for fixed premiums. Choosing and investing in the right term life insurance plan is of paramount importance for anyone with dependents, and the right term life insurance plan offers security and value for money. The premium for the best term insurance plan depends on several crucial factors including age, gender, ppt, contract term, sum insured, etc. chosen by you and term of the policy.
